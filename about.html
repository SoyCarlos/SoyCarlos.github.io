Welcome to Me!

My name is Carlos E. Ortega. I was born and raised in Patterson, CA. For my first two years of life, I lived in a small house in the fields with my parents. There, they owned a 1998 HP Pavillion Desktop computer which was my first introduction to the technology. I played CD-Rom games from McDonald's Happy Meals and made Word documents for fun. Since then, I have been obsessed with the devices and have gone on to college to study software development and data science.
